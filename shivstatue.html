Kachnar City Shiv Statue

Overview

The Kachnar City Shiv Statue in Jabalpur is a towering 76-feet idol of Lord Shiva seated in meditation over a cave. It is not only a divine destination but also an architectural marvel. The statue stands amidst a peaceful garden, and the cave beneath houses replicas of the 12 Jyotirlingas. Devotees and tourists visit this place for spiritual experience and serenity.
