Dhuandhar Waterfall

Overview

Dhuandhar Waterfall is one of the most iconic natural wonders in Jabalpur, named after the smoky mist ('Dhuan') that rises as water crashes down with force. Located on the Narmada River, this majestic waterfall is visible year-round, but becomes especially thunderous during the monsoon season. The nearby ropeway offers breathtaking aerial views, and the sound of the roaring waters can be heard from a distance.
