Pisanhari ki Madiya

Overview

Pisanhari ki Madiya is a revered Jain temple complex situated on a hilltop in Jabalpur. Built over 500 years ago by a poor woman who earned money by grinding grains ("pisen" in Hindi), this temple is a symbol of faith, simplicity, and determination. The serene hill location adds to its spiritual aura.
