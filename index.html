Welcome to Jabalpur

The Marble City of Madhya Pradesh

Developed By – Sumit Bain & Pranjal Patel

Tourist Spots

Bhedaghat

Explore the magnificent marble rocks and enjoy boating in the Narmada River.

Dhuandhar Waterfall

Witness the stunning smoky waterfalls that roar with glory in monsoons.

Madan Mahal Fort

A historic hilltop fort with panoramic views of the city and fascinating history.

Kachnar City Shiv Statue

A massive 76‑feet Lord Shiva statue, a spiritual attraction of Jabalpur.

Bargi Dam

Bargi Dam of Jabalpur is a major water source and scenic boating spot on the Narmada River.

Pisanhari ki Madhiya

Historic Jain temple built by a woman who saved money from grinding grain. A place of peace and devotion.

Gwarighat

Aarti at Narmada riverbank with spiritual significance, cremation grounds, and temples.

Chosath Jogani Temple

Ancient temple from the 10th century, circular design, dedicated to 64 Yoginis powerful divine figures.
