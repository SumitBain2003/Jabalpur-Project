Bhedaghat

Overview

Bhedaghat, located near Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh, is known for its spectacular marble rock formations along the Narmada River. The gorge with white marble cliffs is best viewed via a boat ride, especially on moonlit nights when the rocks glow beautifully. Adjacent to it lies the mighty Dhuandhar Falls, creating a misty roar that fills the valley.
