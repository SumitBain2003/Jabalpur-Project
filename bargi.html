Bargi Dam

Overview

Bargi Dam is one of the first completed dams on the Narmada River and is a popular tourist destination in Jabalpur. Known for its serene reservoir, it offers picturesque views, speed boating, cruise rides, and picnic spots. It is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.
