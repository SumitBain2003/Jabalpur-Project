Gwarighat

Overview

Gwarighat, located on the banks of the holy Narmada River, is a serene and spiritual destination in Jabalpur. It is famous for its evening Narmada Aarti. A vibrant and sacred event where devotees gather to offer prayers with lamps and chants. Gwarighat is also known for its cremation ghat, Ashram temples, and peaceful riverbank, attracting tourists and pilgrims alike.
