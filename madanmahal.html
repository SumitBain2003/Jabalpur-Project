Madan Mahal Fort

Overview

Madan Mahal Fort, built in the 11th century by the Gond ruler Raja Madan Singh, sits atop a hill in Jabalpur and offers panoramic views of the city. The fort is known for its ancient architecture, military strategy rooms, secret passages, and proximity to balancing rocks. A trek to the top is rewarding, with scenic beauty, historical significance, and spiritual vibes from nearby temples.
